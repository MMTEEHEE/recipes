Description

Delicious lasagna recipe with cheese, sauce, ground beef and a fork

Recipe

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt and pepper

STEPS

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Put pasta in a thing that goes into the oven
  3. Put sauce on the layer
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Repeat all previous steps for 3 layers
  6. Cook in over for an hour
