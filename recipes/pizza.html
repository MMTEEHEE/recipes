Description

Delicious pizza recipe with cheese, sauce, pepperoni (optional)

Recipe

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Aregano
  • Parmesan

STEPS

  1. Create the dough and let it rest
  2. Spread the dough and create a circle
  3. Pour sauce on the pizza
  4. Spread the cheese on to pof the pizza
  5. Put in the oven at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
